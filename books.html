Book Recommendations

A list of books and resources on design, strategy and tech. I’ll add more details about why I recommend each book/resource over time.

Books

  • Innovators Dilemma - Professor Christensen
  • Zero to One - Peter Thiel
  • Team of Teams - Stanley McChrystal
  • Blue Ocean Strategy - Chan Kim
  • Radical Help - Hillary Cottam
  • Who Says Elephants Can’t Dance? - Louis Gerstner (IBM's transformation)

Newsletters/Articles

  • Benedict Evans (A16z)
  • Ben Thompson (Stratechery)
  • Learning by shipping
  • No Mercy, No Malice
