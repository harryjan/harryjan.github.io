What I do

I work at the intersection of Business Strategy and Digital Technical Delivery, aligning both so Investments make sense and the Delivery Teams are set up for success.

I tend to work at the leadership level, owning the strategy, building, coaching and enthusing teams. However I will take on Product Manager/Owner roles if the business/technical challenge is sufficiently interesting, but tend to get frustrated with legacy ways of working and want to fix those on the way!

The types of things I’ve done in roles that I’m really good at:

  • Fixing broken things. Teams/processes/programmes that aren’t delivering or are struggling to get going.
  • Starting things; winning business cases, getting products from 0 to V1.0 and building new teams. I am not a “business as usual person”. I need to see progress in what I do, not "turn the handle".
  • Coaching/Mentoring delivery teams and senior leaders, around Product Strategy, Engineering management, and Product Operations.

Specific things I can help with:

  • Product Management - Going from 0 to 1 on Proof of Concepts, MVPs and strategic new builds fast to capture opportunities
  • Product Portfolio Management - Where/how should you invest in product(s) or cut your losses?
  • Digital Investment Cases - How to write, govern and make decisions on them
    • Implementing Stage Gated funding models to take more ‘bets’ whilst managing risk
    • Facilitating Ideation, Hackathons and Proof of concepts (PoCs) to develop and test ideas
    • Coaching/Mentoring technical teams navigating Business concepts & politics (and vice versa)
  • Product/Engineering maturity reviews - Turning Product Investment into high quality product
    • Implementing AI in product/engineering to accelerate delivery and improve quality
    • Implementing Infrastructure-as-Code (IaC) to unblock product teams and scale faster
    • Implementing Secure Cloud Architectures at platform and product level to ensure compliance and security of client data
    • Implementing Agile, DevOps and DevSecOps working practices to build better, more secure products
    • Coaching/Mentoring at delivery and exec levels in Engineering management and scaling Product/engineering teams
  • Planning Product-Led Digital Transformation - How to go from high level vision statements to real change on the ground
    • Programme management - leading change towards Product based business models
    • Programme/Project/Product health checks - Leading retrospectives and Pivot/persevere activities
    • Coaching/Mentoring at delivery and exec levels in Product Led Transformation and effective Business Change to achieve lasting impact

My guiding principles

My definition of digital is: Applying the culture, processes, business models and technologies of the internet-era to respond to people's raised expectations.

Digital does not equal IT or technology on its own.

I’m driven by delivering public good and solving interesting challenges in sectors that impact people's lives, not optimising click-through rates by an additional 5%.

Delivery, delivery, delivery. I’m not interested in theory, academics or endless pontificating. People need things to get better FAST. There’s a lot at stake.
